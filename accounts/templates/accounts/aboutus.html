Time for a brief Introduction


We want development to be fun and add a competitive touch to it, and what can be better than teaming up with the fellas of your own University?

We want to bring every talented engineers/students/developers who have immense potential but do not have any project ideas to work on and get experienced in. Not just that, we also made this website Open Source so that even developers/students can learn how things work at Industrial level.

Create your own projects, make a team, divide the workload and build exciting projects right here at NSP - where ideas come to life!

Well, this website was never intended to be launched as it was just a learning project for Pratik and Rishi. After days of Googling, Stackoverflow , Ctrl+C and Ctrl+V, this is the best we could come up with.

Prachal, the sole FrontEnd Developer of the team sat hours understanding the templates he copied, fortunately he did.

This was our brief introduction. It's time for you to connect with the developers around the globe and start working on your own projects. Your profile page will be your introduction to the world, make sure to make it worth watching.
